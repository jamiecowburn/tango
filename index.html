How to play

Use the English topic clue to guess the hiragana word. Pick a cell, choose a hiragana character, then check your guess.

Green means correct place. Gold means the character is in the answer. Grey means it is not in the answer.

Some practice answers are learning prompts rather than natural Japanese words. Tango tells you when that happens.
