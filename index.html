How to play

Choose hiragana or katakana, then pick a topic from the list. Use the English clue to guess the Japanese word.

Pick a cell, choose a kana character, then check your guess. The on-page chart can stay open as a side-by-side visual aid.

Green means correct place. Gold means the character is in the answer. Grey means it is not in the answer.

Hiragana topics use common Japanese words and phrases. Katakana topics use borrowed words such as fast food, drinks, clothing, and transport.
